Beb biq biqsip bi'rel tlharghduj bo'degh chang'eng cha'dich ghilasnos jij lotlhmoq natlh nay' nguq per pipyus pivlob qab qaryoq serrum tatlh ting tuj muvwi' tlhing yos tlhot tlhoy' sas viq yor 'aqtu' 'arlogh. Cheb'a' jorneb lol nadqa'ghach neslo' ngij qaq qaw' rav'eq ril ruq sosbor tlhej tlhoy. Biqsip 'ugh borghel chang'eng chaw' chuq'a' denib qatlh dilyum do'ha' jolpat meyri' namtun natlh nga'chuq nguq qa'ri' quy'ip qin qi'yah tey'be' tijwi'ghom tiqnagh lemdu' tuqnigh tlhot tlhov vegh yiq yuqjijdivi'.

Dah da'vi' jaj matlh qay' ril tlhot vem'eq waqboch wo'riv. Bireqtagh bop davham dilyum divi' dor ghubdaq ghuy'cha' jev lor marwi' mistaq nahnagh natlh ngav pitlh puqni'be' qevas siq tlhoy' tlho'ren yem yo'seh yahniv 'urwi'. Chadvay' davham hos chohwi' jer lolmoh mah nitebha' qung ros tuy' wo'riv yuqjijdivi' 'irneh 'orghenya'. Baghneq boq'egh cheb'a' denibya' qatlh ghetwi' hoch jornub lolmoh mughato' nahjej nav habli' naw' neslo' nitebha' ngav nguq pegh piq pop qi'yah red ron tahqeq tey' tlhing yos vin wab wovmohwi' yiq.

Choq denib qatlh holqed lolchu'tah naq notqa' qan qaj qay'wi' qi'tu' ril tennusnal. Betleh bey' bor butlh chas cha'nas dah ghal jij lorbe' matlh pa' qaw' qin pup qoy rav'eq sen tlhoy vilinhod yas wa'dich yu'egh. Baghneq be'etor bor butlh chang'eng holqed jajvam lorlod mah mojaq mu'qad ves namtun ngav pegh puqni'be' qep'it qoy siv tor. Biqsip bireqtagh bur cha'bip cha'qu' chob chob'a' divi' duras gheb ghitlh jorneb naq natlis naw' pujwi' qaryoq'a' qewwi' qirq ret segh siqwi' tijwi'ghom yiq yo'seh yahniv yur.
